Greetings all outfit members,

It has been noted that several outfit members would like to transition into the position of a squad leader role. The existing leadership team are delighted at this and would like to provide the opportunity to those who are willing to put in the time and effort to lead a squad.

First off I will be requesting that you meet these minimum requirements;
• Reached the rank of Lieutenant
• Have a microphone with very little distortion
• Excellent communication skills
• Engage in feedback with fellow squad leaders and squad members

The first test is to use one of the following quotes to reply to this thread you may also include additional information, please do not modify the quote just include it in your reply.

If you meet the following requirements please reply to this thread stating
I meet the requirements sign me up
If you are not yet lieutenant rank but would like to take part in the training please reply with
I am working towards lieutenant rank, sign me up
If you do not have a microphone reply
I don't have a microphone what can I do?
